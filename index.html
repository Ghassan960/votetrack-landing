👥 User Roles

Built for every member
of your campaign team

One platform, four powerful roles. Everyone sees exactly what they need.

🏅
Candidate
See your live pledge breakdown, approve field agent updates, track turnout by area, and monitor your team in real time.
🧑‍💼
Field Agent
View assigned voters, submit pledge updates, correct phone numbers, and reassign voters — all from your phone, no app install needed.
🗳️
Booth Operator
Mark voters as voted in real time at the polling station with a fast, searchable voter list. Zero training required.
👁️
Monitor
Read-only live view of voter turnout and activity logs — built for observers, auditors, and transparency oversight.

Everything your campaign needs,
nothing it doesn't.

📊
Live Pledge Tracking
Colour-coded pledge dashboard updated the instant an agent submits. Know who supports which candidate across every area, live.

Master Voter Database

Approval Workflow
Agents submit requests; candidates approve or reject them. Phone corrections write to the database the instant they're approved.
🚀 How It Works

From setup to election day
in 3 steps

1
Build Your Voter List
Import master voter data from Google Sheets. VoteTrack maps every voter by NID, area, and party — ready to filter and assign instantly.
2
Create & Assign
Set up elections, assign field agents to areas, and let the system auto-assign voters. Configure candidate colours and pledge categories your way.
3
Go Live
Booth operators mark votes. Agents update pledges. Candidates watch the live dashboard. Every change syncs across all devices instantly.
