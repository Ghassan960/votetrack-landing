📋 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how VoteTrack ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our application and website.

VoteTrack is a real-time election campaign management platform. Access to the platform is granted exclusively by invitation from an election administrator. The version available on the Apple App Store is the Monitor role only — a read-only view of live voter turnout and election activity. Monitors cannot submit, modify, or transmit any personal data through the app.

By using VoteTrack, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

🗃️ 3. Information We Collect

For the Monitor App (App Store version):

The Monitor app is a read-only dashboard. It requires only your account credentials (provided to you by an election administrator) to authenticate. Beyond authentication, the Monitor app does not collect any personal information from your device.

Data Type Collected? Purpose
Email / Username Yes Authentication only — to verify you are an authorised monitor
Device identifiers No Not collected
Location data No Not collected
Contacts / Camera / Microphone No Not accessed or requested
Usage analytics No Not collected via the Monitor app
Crash reports Optionally via Firebase Crashlytics App stability only — no personal data included
Voter / election data viewed Read-only Displayed to you on screen — not stored on your device

For web platform users (non-App Store): Admin, Candidate, Agent, and Booth Operator roles use the web platform exclusively. These roles may input and manage voter data as part of their campaign responsibilities. All data is stored in Google Firestore and governed by Firebase's privacy terms.

🎯 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and grant access to your assigned role
  • Display real-time election data relevant to your role
  • Maintain app stability (crash diagnostics only, if enabled)

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing purposes
  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Building profiles or tracking behaviour across apps
  • Any purpose unrelated to providing the VoteTrack service

🗓️ 6. Data Retention

Account credentials (email/username) are retained for as long as your access to VoteTrack is active. When an election administrator removes your access, your authentication record is deleted.

Election data (voter records, pledge information, vote counts) is retained by the election administrator for the duration they manage it within the platform. Monitors have no ability to export, download, or retain any data they view.

Crash diagnostics (if collected) are retained for a maximum of 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics, after which they are automatically deleted.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

VoteTrack is not directed at children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 17. Access to VoteTrack is restricted to authorised campaign personnel, all of whom must be adults.

If you believe a child has been granted access in error, please contact us immediately at dave@votetrack.net and we will take immediate action to remove their access.

🔐 8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. These include:

  • Firebase Authentication — industry-standard secure authentication with encrypted credential storage
  • Firestore Security Rules — role-based access control ensuring each user can only access data appropriate to their role
  • HTTPS / TLS encryption — all data in transit is encrypted
  • No local data storage — the Monitor app does not cache or persist any election data on your device

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

⚖️ 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at dave@votetrack.net. We will respond within 30 days.

Note: as the Monitor role does not store personal data on your device beyond your login credentials, most of these rights apply to the authentication record held by Firebase only.
